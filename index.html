Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Baloji's Cafe

What are your operating hours?

Baloji's Cafe is open daily from 2PM to 11PM. We're open 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! We offer free home delivery service in Kudachi. You can order via WhatsApp at +91 9620538708 or call us directly. Minimum order value may apply for delivery.

What cuisines do you serve?

We serve a variety of cuisines including Indian, Chinese, Continental, and Italian. Our menu features Pizza, Burgers, Pasta, Momos, Noodles, Fried Rice, Rolls, Sandwiches, Soups, Quick Bites, Beverages, and Desserts.

Where is Baloji's Cafe located?

Baloji's Cafe is located opposite to Railway Station, near Bus Stop in Kudachi, Karnataka - 591311. We're easily accessible and have convenient parking nearby.

Do you accept reservations?

While we primarily operate on a walk-in basis, you can call us at +91 9620538708 to check table availability, especially during peak hours. We recommend calling ahead for large groups.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, and digital wallets. For online orders, payment can be made via UPI or cash on delivery.

Is your menu vegetarian?

Yes, all our dishes are 100% vegetarian. We offer a wide variety of vegetarian options including veg pizzas, burgers, pasta, momos, and more.
